How to Join BPPA

Simple application process

Becoming a member of the Botswana Payroll Professionals Alliance is easy. Follow these simple steps to start your journey with us.

1

Choose Your Membership Type: Select between Trainee or Professional membership based on your career stage and qualifications.

2

Complete Application Form: Fill out the online application form with your personal and professional details.

3

Submit Required Documents: Provide necessary documentation including proof of qualifications, ID, and professional references.

4

Pay Application Fee: Make payment for the one-time application fee via bank transfer or online payment.

5

Application Review: Your application will be reviewed by the BPPA membership committee within 5-7 business days.

6

Receive Confirmation: Once approved, you'll receive your membership certificate and welcome pack.

7

Pay Annual Membership: Complete your first annual membership payment to activate all benefits.

8

Start Enjoying Benefits: Access all member resources, events, and networking opportunities immediately.
