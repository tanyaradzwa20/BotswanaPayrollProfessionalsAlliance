Membership

\n

To become a member, visit our Membership page and choose the membership type that suits your professional level (Trainee or Professional). Complete the online application form, submit the required documents, and pay the application fee. Once your application is reviewed and approved, you'll receive confirmation and your membership certificate.

We offer two membership tiers:

  • Trainee Membership: BWP 150 application fee + BWP 300 annual fee
  • Professional Membership: BWP 300 application fee + BWP 750 annual fee

Annual fees are renewed each year to maintain active membership status.

Members enjoy numerous benefits including:

  • Access to exclusive training programs and workshops
  • Discounted rates on all BPPA events and seminars
  • Free access to our resource library and publications
  • Professional certification and recognition
  • Networking opportunities with industry professionals
  • Monthly newsletters and industry updates
  • Career development support and mentorship programs

Yes! You can upgrade to Professional membership at any time. You'll need to meet the professional membership requirements (relevant qualifications and work experience) and pay the difference in fees. Contact us for assistance with the upgrade process.

Events & Training

\n

Visit our Events page to view upcoming events. Click on the "Register Now" button for the event you're interested in, complete the registration form, and submit payment. You'll receive a confirmation email with event details and joining instructions.

Yes! BPPA members receive significant discounts on all events. Many webinars and online sessions are completely free for members. Check the event listings for member pricing, which typically offers 30-50% savings compared to non-member rates.

Yes, all our accredited training programs and workshops offer Continuous Professional Development (CPD) points. The number of points varies based on the duration and type of training. Certificates with CPD points are issued upon successful completion of each program.

Many of our events are available in hybrid format, allowing both in-person and virtual attendance. All webinars are conducted online. Check the event details for attendance options. Virtual attendees receive the same materials and certificates as in-person participants.

Resources & Publications

\n

Active BPPA members have full access to our resource library. Simply log in to your member portal and navigate to the Resources section. You can browse by category, search for specific topics, and download materials directly. Some basic resources are also available to non-members.

All resources in our library are free for BPPA members. This includes templates, guides, manuals, publications, and regulatory updates. Non-members may purchase individual resources or join BPPA for unlimited access to all materials.

We update our resource library regularly to ensure all information is current and compliant with the latest regulations. Regulatory updates are posted as soon as changes are announced. Our quarterly newsletter features new resources and industry developments. Members are notified of significant updates via email.

\n

Payments & Billing

\n

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, credit/debit cards, and mobile money (Orange Money, MyZaka). Payment instructions are provided during registration. All transactions are processed securely.

For annual membership fees, we offer a payment plan option allowing you to pay in quarterly installments. Contact our office to arrange a payment plan. Event fees generally require full payment upon registration.

Event cancellations made 7 days or more before the event date receive a full refund minus a 10% processing fee. Cancellations within 7 days receive a 50% refund. Membership fees are non-refundable but can be transferred to the following year with administrative approval.
